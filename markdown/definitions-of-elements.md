An example; the repulsive force equals .281 (column 12), therefore, .281 = 28.1% (column 10). The weakness of the nucleus is 28.1%.

Should a nucleus have a weakness of 28.1%, then it must have the strength of the remaining balance. therefore, 100% minus 28.1% equals 71.9% (column 9).
